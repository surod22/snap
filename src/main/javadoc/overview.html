snap is a scalable thread-safe {@link com.lambdaworks.snap.PushNotificationClient client} for Apple's Push Notification Service. Outgoing notifications are queued and sent asynchronously via a persistent connection to the notification gateway.

A {@link com.lambdaworks.snap.PushNotificationClient PushNotificationClient} is designed to be long-lived, and if the connection is lost will reconnect until {@link com.lambdaworks.snap.PushNotificationClient#shutdown shutdown} is called. Pending notifications will be (re)sent afer successful reconnection. PushNotificationClient will automatically connect to the APNS Feedback Service at a configurable interval and deliver any feedback notifications to all registered {@link com.lambdaworks.snap.FeedbackListener listeners}.
